Number of Public Companies 1970-2023

History of listings on NYSE, AMEX & NASDAQ

Published: August 6, 2025

Tags: Coding, Stock Market

By Luke Buck

Ever wonder how many companies are publicly traded? Turns out there has been a steady decline in the number of public companies since 1997. See below for a historical count of publicly listed companies on the NYSE, AMEX and NASDAQ exchanges.

Note that these results include corporations and real-estate investment trusts (REITs) from the three major american historical exchanges. Additionally, only companies with a common share class were included.

To my surprise, the number of publicly traded companies hasn't exceeded ten thousand in the last 60+ years. Note the introduction of the NASDAQ exchange in late 1972 doubled publicly traded companies.

I threw this plot together quickly using Python and market data from Compustat and CRSP.

And finally, no I did not use AI to write this article!
